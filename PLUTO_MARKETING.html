✦ Simple Setup

Up and running
in under 10 minutes.

No IT team. No onboarding calls. No 30-day trials that expire before you see value. Pluto gets out of your way so you can focus on the phones.

1
Connect your Google Sheet
Paste your Google Apps Script Web App URL into Settings. Pluto links to your Sheet and sets up the required columns automatically. Your data, your control — forever.
2
Add your leads and build pipelines
Create pipeline stages that match your sales process. Add leads manually, import from your Sheet, or paste from a CSV. Customise tags, values, and contact info.
3
Launch a sprint and start calling
Select the leads you want to call today, start a sprint session, and work through your list. Every call outcome syncs to your Sheet automatically.
